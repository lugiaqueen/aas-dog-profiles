Key Finding

The Behavior section on Adopets is empty for every single dog reviewed. Energy Level and Housebroken status are the two fields most consistently missing, and are also the fields most influential in adoption match decisions. Dogs with rich bios (Griselda, Bronx, Zoe, Bandit) still lack structured attribute fields.

Recommended Actions
  • Prioritize behavior section for long-stay dogs first
  • Add "energy level" as a required field in intake workflow
  • Auto-flag profiles with empty bios after 30 days
  • Tag UPL dogs with LOS banner on public profile
