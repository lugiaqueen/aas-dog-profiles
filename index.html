Key Finding

The Behavior section on Adopets is empty for every single dog reviewed. Energy Level and Housebroken are the most consistently missing fields — and the most influential in adoption match decisions. 8 dogs have zero photos, which is a critical barrier to engagement before anyone even reads the bio.

Recommended Actions
  • Add at least 3 photos for all zero-photo dogs first
  • Prioritize behavior fields for UPL and long-stay dogs
  • Add energy level as a required field at intake
  • Auto-flag profiles with empty bios after 30 days in shelter
Field Definitions

Dog-Friendly — Compatibility with other dogs (EWD/D2D tags)
Kid-Friendly — Compatibility with children (EWOK/EWYK tags)
Housebroken — Housetrained status (HT tag)
Energy Level — Activity and exercise needs documented
Photos — Count of images on the public profile
LOS — Length of Stay (LOS tag auto-triggers at 12 mo+)
